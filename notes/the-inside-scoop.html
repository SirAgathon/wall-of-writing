    inside scoop

    Beneath the glaze, an icy facade gleams,

    A shallow taste, like fleeting, whispered dreams.

    Yet, venturing beyond the surface lore,

    Uncovers depths, where veiled truths soar.

    As igloos guard the warmth against the gale,

    The outer crust faces the biting veil.

    With each delve deeper into the frost's embrace,

    Revealed are gems, in hidden, hallowed space.

    Beyond mere cream, a realm sublime,

    With chunks of dough and brownie, time

    Entwined within the frost's serene delight,

    A hidden trove, veiled from the sight.

    As life unfurls, beyond the sheen, define

    The essence true, in depth's design.
