    • Mandalas

    Mandalas are a Buddhist symbol, typically made with concentric circles or rectangles. They could be considered expressions of art, yet they are also used as an aid for meditation. The concentricness of the geometries serves as a guide for the meditator.

    How to Use

    Creating mandalas is a highly contemplative and relaxing activity. You can download my mandalas and trace over them. You can also add colors and other decorations as you wish.

    Under the Creative Commons BY-SA license, my mandalas are free to use as long as you attribute any subsequent publishings to me (Jeff K Wang). For more information, visit https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/ .  

    Note: While the origins of mandalas are Buddhist, my mandalas, as with the other content on my website, are secular. The mandalas depict no religious content – spiritual, maybe.
