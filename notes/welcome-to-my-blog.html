    About Me

    At the time of writing this, I am a young upstart – just 23 years of age. I come from Fremont, California, in the heart of Silicon Valley. My father and mother immigrated from Taiwan and Mainland China, respectively. My father is a wholesale trader, and my mother is a mammographer at a non-profit clinic. I am the eldest child, with only a younger brother, trailing me by three years. For college, I initially attended University of California, Riverside and, then, transferred to the University of Southern California. I graduated in August 2023 with a bachelor’s degree in economics and mathematics. Currently, I am seeking a job, whether it be working for myself or starting a company or working for someone else. In other words, I am still figuring things out.

    Motivation

    I’ve already had a running blog – a shabby one, that is. I am creating this blog to improve upon that one.

    The main reason why I embarked upon this project is to do things that don’t scale. This blog is created piece-by-piece, and every page is put together with scrutiny. Craftsmanship – that is what I am getting at here.

    My pleasure in having a blog comes from the art of writing. To me, there is no medium like a piece of paper and a pen. My mind flows that way.

    I also do appreciate having people taking an interest to my life. Through my writings, I hope you will be able to understand me better.
