    • On the corner of Coral and Vine,

    I often find myself facing the same ten-second countdown… 10, 9.

    Depending on the time of day I am out and about,

    8, 7 … sometimes I see a weary face – friendly, but weary.

    6, 5 … again, I find myself walking this same route.

    4 … assure you, am merry

    Yet, I find myself wondering:

    Why we do the things we do

    As I walk into the intersection, it hits me.

    Two passersby, just crossing the street –

    A strut and a swing … 3, 2

    A part of an unfolding dance

    0, 1, and STOP
